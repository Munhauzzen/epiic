Why epiic?

Driven
by simplicity

You get a team of people working on your designs for a fraction of the costs. We recruit top talent, review design submissions for quality, ensure deadlines are met no matter what, and manage backup talent.

  • All plans come with a 30-day money back guarantee.
  • Our customer support team is always on hand to help.

Powered
by talent

We are a team of fully committed, in-house design addicts that cover a range of skills from illustrations to animations and website design.

  • Our strict review process ensures we hire only 2% of our applicants.
  • A senior designer reviews every project before you see it.
  • We don’t do generic. We ensure our designs are fresh, unique, and current.

Enabled
by technology

With a decade of experience working with businesses and agencies we’ve crafted a platform to cover all bases. It’s powerful, yet intuitive.

  • Consolidate all brand assets and resources in one place.

  • Customize workflows with integrations via API, Zapier, and Slack.
