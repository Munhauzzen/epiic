How it works

Save more time + get more value.
It’s the epiic way.

Choose a subscription

Choose blogging or writing, with options ranging from self-serviced to fully managed plans.

Order content

Share your project’s details with your writer. If you’re not sure how to create a brief, we can help.

Review and approve

Easily invite clients or teammates to review the content. Request free revisions if necessary or approve.

The epiic process is engineered to make your life easier.
Here’s how we do it.

Transparent pricing

All costs are upfront.
Pay a flat fee each month; that’s it.

Editor review

Our editors review each project to ensure it’s polished. They check for quality, originality, and adherence to the brief before you even look at it.

No limit on revisions

We’re not happy until you are.
That’s why we let you request as many revisions as you need.

100% original

Enjoy worry-free content. Our editors check for plagiarism, and they run everything through Copyscape, the industry standard for duplicate content detection.

Customized
writer matching

We’ll match you with a writer who has an in-depth understanding of your industry, your business niche, and how to resonate with your audience.

Total support

We’re here to help. Our support team helps you with everything from writer matching to project management to ensure your project meets your expectations.

Easy communication

Keep all your communications in one place. Our platform is set up so you can message your writer directly from your project page.

Writer flexibility

Want a different writer? No problem.
You can switch writers in one click.

No contracts

You can cancel your subscription at any time.
No fees, no strings attached.

30-day money
back guarantee

You can try epiic writers worry-free with our 30-day money back guarantee. If you’re not happy with our service, simply cancel your subscription and we’ll return everything.

  • Define roles for your team members and clients

All that, powered by
native English-speaking writers

All of our writers are experienced in the craft of content
writing. We follow a stringent vetting process to ensure
we hire only the best.

Our team sifts through hundreds of writer applications each month.

After passing a pre-screening test, applicants go through two layers of human review.

On average, we accept fewer than 2% of writer applicants. Only native English speakers are allowed on the platform.

Our editing team reviews all projects for quality and uses an internal feedback system to ensure consistency.

We also work with writers to help them improve, providing feedback so they can boost their skills.
