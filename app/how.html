Technology is what makes us epiic

We’ve built a feature-rich platform to make this ultra-simple process possible. It offers communication, project and talent management, and automations – all through a single user interface. And, it makes scaling a breeze.

Unlimited brand profiles

  • Create unlimited profiles for your brands or clients.
  • Optimize workflow by storing brand-related assets, making them available for all future requests.

Unlimited users

  • Create logins and define roles for individual users.
  • Maintain granular control over what your teammates or stakeholders access.

Free access to 20,000,000+ stock images

Collaboration tools for agencies

We’ll be blunt: our designers are brilliant.
Here’s how we find them.

We review hundreds of applications every month.

Each applicant goes through a pre-screening process, including a test
