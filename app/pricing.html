You may still have some questions,
so here's the FAQ:

  • What does unlimited graphic design mean?
  • You get access to our award-winning design team for everything from website graphics and animated GIFs to printed materials and packaging. Submit your graphic design request and receive your custom design within 1 business day. Repeat as many times as you need.
  • What is the turnaround time?
  • Usually, our designers will have your request back to you within 1 business day. Complex requests such as a full website design, large illustration sets, and pitch decks take longer. Turnaround times are impacted by:
    • The complexity of the design
    • How many of your requests we’re currently working on
    • How clear your instructions are
  • Will you design my whole website in 1 day?
  • Basic graphic design tasks take, on average, 1 business day to complete, however tasks like website design, presentation decks, and infographics are more complex, so they take more time to be completed. You’ll receive a first draft or wireframe of a page within the business day.
  • How does the queue work?
  • We’ll complete one of your designs at a time before starting the next, two at a time with our Double plan, and more with a custom plan. However, you can place several requests at once if you want. If you need to prioritize the order of your projects, simply drag your design requests into the correct order on your project list page.
  • What’s the difference between the Pro, Pro+ and Double plans?
  • All plans include unlimited design services. However, they do vary by the types of design work available and the number of designers who will work on your projects at once. View our scope of work to see exactly what each plan includes.
    • With the Pro plan, you can request banner ads, infographics, and other simple design projects. It’s a great option if you have an in-house design team and need help with an overflow of projects, or if you are a small business that requires ongoing designs for digital marketing purposes.
    • With Pro+, ideal for businesses that need full design services, your subscription includes most common design projects: landing pages, web design, animated GIFs, logos, and more.
    • The Double plan is great for agencies and businesses that need a large volume of work completed in a limited amount of time. You’ll always have two designers working on your projects.
  • What if I want more than 2 designers?
  • No problem! Whether you’re a large creative agency or a business with high-volume design needs, our design team is here for you. To get started,schedule a discovery call with our epiic sales team and we’ll create a custom subscription that serves your unique graphic design needs.
  • What if I dislike a design?
  • If you receive a design that you’re unhappy with, request a revision and our design team will improve it, whether you need a few changes, a different style, or a complete do-over. Our platform lets you click on any part of the design you want changed so you can add comments and let the designer know what you want done. We prioritize revisions so you’ll get yours back fast.

    You never have to worry about being stuck with a design you don’t like. We offer unlimited revisions, and you can switch your designer at any time with one click.
  • What is the 30-day money back guarantee?
  • We offer a full refund for your subscription within the first 30 days after your start date. If you don’t like the designs we created for you, let us know and we’ll give you your money back. It’s that simple.
  • Am I locked into a long-term contract?
  • Nope. Contracts are too limiting so we don’t use them. Our monthly subscription plans can be paused or canceled at any time if you don’t want to renew yours.
  • Who owns the rights to the designs?
  • They’re all yours! You retain 100% ownership of the designs once you approve them. This includes the actual design and the source files. If you want to change the designs in the future, go ahead. You have the freedom to use them however you want.
  • What does unlimited brands mean?
  • You can use your unlimited design subscription for as many different brands or visual identities as you want. This means you can request designs for your own customers, multiple business ventures, or anything else. Because we offer a while label solution, you can also use our platform with your branding, not epiic’s, and present it as your own in-house design service to your customers.
